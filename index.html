  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)

Location Services

The Application collects your device's location, which helps the Service Provider determine your approximate geographical location and make use of in below ways:

  • Geolocation Services: The Service Provider utilizes location data to provide features such as personalized content (e.g., precise prayer times), relevant recommendations, and location-based services.
  • Analytics and Improvements: Aggregated and anonymized location data helps the Service Provider to analyze user behavior, identify trends, and improve the overall performance and functionality of the Application.

Camera Access (Halal Scanner)

For a better experience, while using our Service, the Application may require you to provide us with access to your device’s camera. This permission is specifically used for the Halal Scanner feature, which enables the scanning of product barcodes and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for food ingredient analysis.

The images captured by the camera are processed in real-time to identify the halal status of products. We do not store these images permanently on our servers, nor do we share them with any third parties.
